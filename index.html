Khawja Imran Masud is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET), Gazipur, Bangladesh. He received his M.Sc. Engineering degree in Computer Science and Engineering from DUET. As earlier, he received his B.Sc. Engineering degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the same university.He received the Prime Minister Gold Medal 2018 and the University Gold Medal 2018 for securing the first-class first position overall at DUET in the year 2018. He is an instructor at Cisco Network Academy, DUET. His current research interests include Machine Learning and IoT in the field of intelligence systems.
